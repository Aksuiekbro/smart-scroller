SmartScroller

Only see Shorts, Reels, and videos that match your topics.

Filtering

Turn off to see everything. Use Pause for short breaks.

Feed steering

SmartScroller never auto-watches videos. It steers the feed with local matching, user-controlled avoid lists, and optional YouTube "Not interested" nudges. With auto-steer on, off-topic YouTube cards are queued and a bar lets you send the nudges in one tap — nothing is sent automatically.

Where it runs

Learning topics

In Focus mode, a video is shown if any learning keyword appears in its title, channel, description, or hashtags. Single words match whole words; multi-word phrases match as substrings. Case-insensitive.

Avoid topics

Avoid topics always win. Add channels, phrases, or categories that pull you into low-value loops.
